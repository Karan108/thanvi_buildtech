Fly City

Fly City offers 2 BHK flats ranging from 730 Sq.ft to 1430 Sq.ft areas and it is 10 Minutes Drive from Devanahalli International Airport. Every detail is meticulously done for an exciting life. The Serenity of Devanahalli Town provides the perfect backdrop for modern living. Close to the aspirations of discerning customers, Fly City is coming up as a prestigious residential project in Devanahalli Town, in proximity to the best of Bengaluru North. The Two-Tower magnificent destination promises on integrated wholesome living to a new age clientele. Every apartment blurs the distinction between design and aesthetics. They are detailed to uncompromising standards for quality spaces. Excellent cross ventilation and sunshine of every step invigorate living. You will find all brands of fixtures that elevate your life and style. The integrated environment of Fly City is all about style, and open spaces.
